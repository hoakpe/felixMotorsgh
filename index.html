About Felix Engineering Motors

Founded in 2010, Felix Engineering Motors has grown from a small local garage to one of the most trusted auto repair service providers in the region. Our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction sets us apart.

We use only the highest quality parts and the latest diagnostic equipment to ensure your vehicle receives the best possible care. Our certified technicians undergo regular training to stay updated with the latest automotive technologies.
